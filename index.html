About

I am a Research and Development Software Engineer in the Dell Technologies Office of the CTO Dojo. We research emerging technologies and build prototypes to serve as proofs of concept. I'm an evangelist of Extreme Programming practices which we follow, so feel free to contact me if you want to learn more!

Prior to working at Dell I was a Research Scientist at Feature Labs where I worked on the open source project Featuretools. You can also check my Github page for a nice cross section of my interests, or the programming page on the sidebar.

Mathematics

I studied in algebraic groups over finite fields of positive characteristic. I completed my Ph.D. in 2017 under George McNinch at Tufts University in Medford, MA.

My thesis work involved constructing unipotent groups by group extensions, and then finding representations of nice groups which contain those extensions. My math at this point is a little rusty, but If you happen to be interested in representations of unipotent algebraic groups I can likely still point you in the right direction.
